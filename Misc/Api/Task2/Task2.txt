## **Task 2: Product & Inventory API (Relational Modeling)**

### **Objective**

Manage products with stock control.

### **Entities**

- `Product`
- `Category`

### **Endpoints**

- `POST /categories`
- `POST /products`
- `GET /products?category_id=&min_price=&max_price=`
- `PATCH /products/{id}/stock`

### **Validation Rules**

- Price must be > 0
- Stock must be >= 0
- Category must exist before assigning product
- Product name must be unique per category

### **Advanced Validation**

- Custom Pydantic validators
- Cross-field validation (price vs discount)
